Fried Rice

Description

Inspired by The Recipe Critic

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or frying pan is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It can be eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • White long grain rice
  • Small white onion
  • Small vegetables: peas and carrots
  • Eggs
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Green onions
  • Protein: chicken, sausage, shrimp

Steps

  1. Preheat the skillet with medium high heat and pour sesame oil into the bottom of the skillet.
  2. Add onion, peas, and carrots, and fry until they are tender
  3. Add cooked meat in and mix with veggies.
  4. Move all ingredients in pan to the side and add egg to the other side. Scramble the egg
  5. Once egg is scrambled to taste, mix it with other ingredients
  6. Add the rice and mix in with the mixture.
  7. Pour soy sauce on top and stir until it is heated throughout
  8. Garnish with green onions.
