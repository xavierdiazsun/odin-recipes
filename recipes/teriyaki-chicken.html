Teriyaki Chicken

Description

This is my go to recipe to get large amounts of chicken in, fast, and with little effort.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breat or chicken thigh
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, or any spice you like
  • Oil
  • Teriyaki sauce that you can find at the supermarket.

Steps

  1. Slice chicken into small cubes.
  2. Coat chicken with the spices you enjoy to your taste.
  3. Heat pan with oil.
  4. Put chicken in, and cook on all sides.
  5. Place chicken to the side and pour in teriyaki sauce.
  6. Once teriyaki sauce is bubbling, pur chicken back in and mix.
  7. Serve with steamed vegetables and white rice.
